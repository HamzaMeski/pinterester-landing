Autopilot Content Engine

An internal Pinterest automation tool designed to help creators generate high-quality visual content and schedule pins directly to their authorized boards.

Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

Autopilot Content Engine only accesses data for the authenticated user's Pinterest account. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data.

Data Collection & Usage

We do not store, sell, or share user data with third parties. Authentication tokens are kept securely and used solely for the purpose of publishing the user's own authorized content to their selected boards.

Pinterest API Compliance

This application complies with Pinterest's Developer Guidelines. It utilizes the official API to facilitate automated publishing without circumventing any platform limits or engaging in spam behavior.
