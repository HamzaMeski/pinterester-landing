Privacy Policy

Last Updated: June 2026

VisualPilot ("we," "our," or "us") is committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring the security of your data. This policy explains how our Pinterest automation application handles your information.

1. Data Access via OAuth

VisualPilot operates with strict data isolation. We only access data for the authenticated user's Pinterest account via official OAuth integrations. The application requests the minimum required permissions (scopes) to perform automated pinning on your behalf.

2. Zero Data Selling

We do not store, sell, or share user data with any third parties. Authentication tokens (OAuth refresh tokens) are encrypted and kept securely on our servers, and are used solely for the purpose of publishing your authorized content to your selected Pinterest boards.

3. Pinterest API Compliance

This application complies fully with Pinterest's Developer Guidelines. It utilizes the official API to facilitate automated publishing without circumventing any platform rate limits or engaging in spam behavior. We respect Pinterest's community standards.

4. Data Deletion

Users may revoke VisualPilot's access to their Pinterest account at any time directly through their Pinterest account settings. To request full deletion of your authentication tokens from our servers, please contact our support team.
